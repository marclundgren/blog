# Blog Writing Guidelines

## Voice and tone

When writing blog posts, do not use first-person opinion framing. Avoid phrases like:

- "I think..."
- "The most challenging bit for me was..."
- "I'd normally consider..."
- "It seemed like..."
- "I found it..."
- "For me, X was..."

Factual first-person narrative is fine ("I built X", "The Pi had two interfaces"). What to avoid is subjective opinion or personal assessment language — anything that frames a judgment or feeling as the author's opinion rather than stating it as fact.

Instead of opinion framing, state things directly:
- "I'd normally consider out of scope" → "would normally be out of scope"
- "I thought it was the best approach" → "it was the right approach"
- "The hardest part for me was..." → "The tricky part was..."
